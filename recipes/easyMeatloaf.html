Eaasy Meatloaf

Description:

Easy meatloaf is a simple, comforting dish made with ground meat, breadcrumbs, and seasonings. It’s perfect for a quick family dinner, requiring minimal ingredients and effort.

The meat mixture is shaped into a loaf and baked, often topped with ketchup or glaze for extra flavor. It’s versatile, allowing for easy customization with various vegetables and spices.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup dried bread crumbs
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • ⅓ cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons prepared mustard

How to Make Easy Meatloaf

  1. Mix the loaf ingredients, then transfer to a loaf pan.
  2. Mix the sauce ingredients, then pour over the loaf.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the meatloaf is no longer pink in the center.
